My Watch Collection

FNGEEN Automatic

My first ever watch, an Fngeen automatic, was a birthday gift from my brother. This marked the beginning of my love affair with watches, with its sleek design and auto-wind capability making it both fashionable and functional.

CASIO G-Shock GAS100B-7A

My second watch, which I bought at JCPenney, stood out because of its sleek white design. I enjoyed how it matched my white Air Forces, adding a clean, stylish touch to my outfit. It was not just a time-telling mechanism, but an everyday accessory that easily married fashion and function.

CASIO MDV106(Duro) Series

On a friend's recommendation, I chose my third watch, and I was immediately in love with the striking blue face. The deep, dark color provided that extra something, a fresh and trendy spin for my collection. It was my first choice to wear.

CASIO AE-1200WH

CASIO Edifice EFV-630D-3A

Oruss

Joseph Abboud AB/5178ASV

Joseph Abboud AB/5072RT

Apple Watch: Series 10

My Apple Watch is the gem of my exercise routines, tracking my heart rate, calories, and actual progress. It keeps me hyped up with reminders to exercise and lets me control music without distraction. Whether weightlifting or cardio, it helps me maintain concentration and perform at my best.
